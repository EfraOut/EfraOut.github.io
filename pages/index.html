The Book of Mormon Experiment

Lots of people have heard of the Book of Mormon, but few people have actually read it. We want to change that, one page at a time. The Book of Mormon Experiment is designed for you to read a single page from the most criticized book in religion history, and then tell us (and the world!) what you thought about it.

Because the Book of Mormon speaks for itself

If the Book of Mormon is what it claims to be, then it's not necessary for people to tell you what it is, but you should be able to experience it by yourself. More info on the official site can be found here.
