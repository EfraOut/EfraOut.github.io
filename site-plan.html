Overview

Purpose

When I say, “The Book of Mormon”, what is the first thing that comes to your mind? The Book of Mormon is one of the most criticized books in religious history, but few have read it. My website is designed to help with that problem. The Book of Mormon Experiment gives the opportunity to people to read a page, highlight what they find interesting, and experience by themselves the power of the Book of Mormon. This is how I plan to correct misconceptions and give everyone a clear chance to decide.

Audience

This website is for everyone! People who have read the Book of Mormon would participate in this social experiment the same way that someone who hasn't. Whether someone is trying to find out what the Book of Mormon is or if they want to read it with a new perspective, this website provides the needed tools. Since this page is in English, it will target all the English speakers.

Content

Home page

The Book of Mormon Experiment: Lots of people have heard of the Book of Mormon, but few people have actually read it. We want to change that, one page at a time. The Book of Mormon Experiment is designed for you to read a single page from the most criticized book in religion history, and then tell us (and the world!) what you thought about it. Because the Book of Mormon speaks for itself: If the Book of Mormon is what it claims to be, then it's not necessary for people to tell you what it is, but you should be able to experience it by yourself. More info on the official site can be found here.
